> **The most advanced automation tool for Your Bizarre Adventure** — featuring auto-farming, quest automation, stand farming, PvP enhancements, and a modern WindUI interface.

---

##  Features

###  Core Automation
- **Auto Item Farming** — Intelligent item collection with ESP and instant pickup
- **Quest Farm** — Automatic quest completion with progressive leveling (up to Level 35+)
- **Stand Farm** — Farm for specific stands or any shiny variant
- **Rib Farm** — Automated Saint's Corpse rib cage farming
- **Auto Sell** — Smart inventory management with max-cap detection

###  Movement & Combat
- **Multiple Travel Methods** — Stud, Tween, and Instant teleportation
- **Infinite Dash** — Customizable dash power and delay
- **Fly Mode** — Full flight control with adjustable speed
- **Noclip** — Collision bypass for farming
- **Speed Modifier** — Undetectable speed adjustments

###  PvP & Trolling
- **Player ESP** — Real-time player highlighting and tracking
- **Sticker** — Attach to players/mobs with various modes (Back, Up, Down)
- **View Player** — Spectate any player in the server
- **Stand Pilot** — Control your stand remotely with infinite range
- **Invisibility** — Advanced invisibility with keybind toggle

###  Utility & Protection
- **Anti-AFK** — Prevents automatic kicks
- **FPS Booster** — Performance optimization for low-end devices
- **Auto Rejoin** — Automatic reconnection on kick/disconnect
- **Sound Notifier** — Audio alerts for specific item spawns
- **Panic Kick** — Self-kick on developer detection (client-side)

###  Gambling & Shopping
- **Auto Gamble** — Automated item machine usage
- **Auto Buy Lucky Arrows** — Smart purchasing when money threshold reached
- **Shop Automation** — One-click purchases for all items and specs

###  SBR (Steel Ball Run)
- **Auto SBR** — Automated race completion with barrier bypass
- **Horse Controls** — Custom walk speed and jump power
- **Teleport System** — Quick access to all SBR locations
